Steps to Make a Sandwich

  1. Take two slices of bread.
  2. Spread butter or mayonnaise on one side of each slice.
  3. Add your favorite fillings, like cheese, lettuce, or ham.
  4. Put the slices together to form a sandwich.
  5. Cut it in half and enjoy!
