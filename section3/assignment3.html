Designing for Simplicity: Tips for Clean UI

Sept 21, 2025 • 5 min read

Start with clarity: prioritize content, remove distractions, and use consistent spacing. Focused typography and restrained color palettes help readers scan quickly.

Use components: cards, lists, and clear headings let users find information fast. Test on small screens first — mobile constraints force you to prioritize what matters.
